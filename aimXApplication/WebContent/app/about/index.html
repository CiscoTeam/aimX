About

AimX is about creating a scalable automated interface for homes/buildings everywhere With this technology anyone would be able to monitor and customize their home to their preferences. With the Internet of Things and home appliances

Smart monitoring would allow for the homes power, water, and A/C data to all be preform optimally

Automation is as simple as configuring options such as: turning on the exterior lights when the sun goes down or turning off irrigation when it starts to rain.
